Gateway of India

I had visited in December 2019.

It is located in Mumbai and was built to welcome King-Emperor George V and Queen-Empress Mary. When I visited the Gateway of India, it was a full moon night and I could see the stars shining brightly in the sky. It was a beutiful sight and I hope you will go there too!

India Gate

I had visited in November 2016.

It is located in Delhi and was formerly known as Kingsway. It looks really good and the lighting during the night, gives it another effect
